About Us

IIITH Mental Health is a group of counsellors and volunteers who help you to get through your tough times.

Services

What We Do.

At IIITH Mental Health, we provide compassionate support for your mental health. Our experienced team offers therapy sessions, resources, and self-care tools tailored to your needs. Whether you're dealing with anxiety, depression, or stress, we're here to listen and guide you towards healing and personal growth. You don't have to face it alone - take the first steps towards a happier, healthier life with us.

Counselling

Our professional counsellors can help you to get through your tough times.

Friends

If you feel shy talking to the counsellors, you can talk to our volunteers who are always there to help you and might be able to relate to your problems better than the counsellors.
